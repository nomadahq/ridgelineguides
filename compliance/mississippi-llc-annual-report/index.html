Mississippi LLC Annual Report: Deadline, Cost, and Filing Guide (2026)

Last updated: February 2026 · Applies to: Mississippi LLCs

Mississippi LLCs must file an annual report by April 15 each year. At $25, the fee is very low.

Quick summary

ItemDetails
FilingAnnual Report
FrequencyEvery year
Due dateApril 15
Filing fee$25
Late feeAdministrative dissolution after continued non-filing
Where to fileMississippi Secretary of State (sos.ms.gov)
Consequence of non-filingAdministrative dissolution

When is it due?

The annual report is due April 15 each year.

How much does it cost?

The filing fee is $25.

How to file

Filing steps

Go to Mississippi SOS Business
Search for your LLC
File the annual report
Pay the $25 fee
Save confirmation

What happens if you miss the deadline?

Continued failure to file leads to administrative dissolution.

Do you need a registered agent in Mississippi?

Yes. Mississippi requires all LLCs to maintain a registered agent in Mississippi.

Need a registered agent in Mississippi?

A registered agent keeps your LLC in good standing and ensures you receive all legal and state documents on time.

Frequently asked questions

Is the fee really only $25?

Yes. Mississippi has one of the lowest annual report fees.

Can I file online?

Yes. File through the Mississippi Secretary of State business portal.

What happens if I am dissolved?

You can reinstate by filing overdue reports and paying associated fees.
