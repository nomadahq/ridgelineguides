West Virginia LLC Annual Report: Deadline, Cost, and Filing Guide (2026)

Last updated: February 2026 · Applies to: West Virginia LLCs

West Virginia LLCs must file an annual report by July 1 each year. The fee is $25.

Quick summary

ItemDetails
FilingAnnual Report
FrequencyEvery year
Due dateJuly 1
Filing fee$25
Late feeAdministrative dissolution after non-filing
Where to fileWest Virginia Secretary of State (sos.wv.gov)
Consequence of non-filingAdministrative dissolution

When is it due?

The annual report is due July 1 each year for all West Virginia LLCs.

How much does it cost?

The annual report fee is $25.

How to file

Filing steps

Go to WV SOS Business
Search for your LLC
File the annual report
Pay the $25 fee
Save confirmation

What happens if you miss the deadline?

Failure to file leads to administrative dissolution.

Do you need a registered agent in West Virginia?

Yes. West Virginia requires all LLCs to maintain a registered agent with a West Virginia address.

Need a registered agent in West Virginia?

A registered agent keeps your LLC in good standing and ensures you receive all legal and state documents on time.
