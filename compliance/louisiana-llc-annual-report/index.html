Louisiana LLC Annual Report: Deadline, Cost, and Filing Guide (2026)

Last updated: February 2026 · Applies to: Louisiana LLCs

Louisiana LLCs must file an annual report each year by the anniversary of formation. The fee is $35, making it one of the more affordable states.

When is it due?

Your annual report is due by the anniversary of your LLC formation each year.

How much does it cost?

The annual report fee is $35.

How to file

Filing steps

Go to Louisiana SOS CORA
Search for your LLC
File the annual report
Pay the $35 fee
Save confirmation

What happens if you miss the deadline?

Failure to file leads to administrative dissolution. Reinstatement requires filing all overdue reports and paying fees.

Do you need a registered agent in Louisiana?

Yes. Louisiana requires all LLCs to maintain a registered agent with a Louisiana street address.

Need a registered agent in Louisiana?

A registered agent keeps your LLC in good standing and ensures you receive all legal and state documents on time.

Frequently asked questions

When is my anniversary date?

The month and day your LLC was originally formed in Louisiana.

Can I file online?

Yes. File through the Louisiana Secretary of State CORA system.

What information is required?

Business name, registered agent, principal address, and member information.
