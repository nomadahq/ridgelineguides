South Dakota LLC Annual Report: Deadline, Cost, and Filing Guide (2026)

Last updated: February 2026 · Applies to: South Dakota LLCs

South Dakota LLCs must file an annual report each year. The fee is $50. South Dakota has no state income tax, making the annual report one of the few compliance requirements.

Quick summary

ItemDetails
FilingAnnual Report
FrequencyEvery year
Due dateFirst day of the anniversary month of formation
Filing fee$50
Late feeAdministrative dissolution after non-filing
Where to fileSouth Dakota Secretary of State (sdsos.gov)
Consequence of non-filingAdministrative dissolution

When is it due?

The annual report is due by the first day of the anniversary month of your LLC formation.

How much does it cost?

The annual report fee is $50.

How to file

Filing steps

Go to SD SOS Enterprise
Search for your LLC
File the annual report
Pay the $50 fee
Save confirmation

What happens if you miss the deadline?

Failure to file leads to administrative dissolution.

Do you need a registered agent in South Dakota?

Yes. South Dakota requires all LLCs to maintain a registered agent with a South Dakota address.

Need a registered agent in South Dakota?

A registered agent keeps your LLC in good standing and ensures you receive all legal and state documents on time.

Frequently asked questions

Does South Dakota have income tax?

No. South Dakota has no state income tax.

Can I file online?

Yes. File through the SD SOS Enterprise portal.

When is my deadline?

The first day of the month your LLC was originally formed.
