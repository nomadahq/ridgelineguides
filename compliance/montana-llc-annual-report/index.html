Montana LLC Annual Report: Deadline, Cost, and Filing Guide (2026)

Last updated: February 2026 · Applies to: Montana LLCs

Montana LLCs must file an annual report by April 15 each year. At $20, Montana has one of the lowest annual report fees in the country.

Quick summary

When is it due?

The annual report is due April 15 each year.

How much does it cost?

The filing fee is $20 — among the lowest in the country.

How to file

Filing steps

Go to Montana SOS Business
Search for your LLC
File the annual report
Pay the $20 fee
Save confirmation

What happens if you miss the deadline?

Failure to file leads to administrative dissolution.

Do you need a registered agent in Montana?

Yes. Montana requires all LLCs to maintain a registered agent with a Montana address.

Need a registered agent in Montana?

A registered agent keeps your LLC in good standing and ensures you receive all legal and state documents on time.

Frequently asked questions

Does Montana have state income tax?

Montana has no sales tax but does have state income tax.

Can I file online?

Yes. File through the Montana Secretary of State business portal.

What is the penalty for late filing?

There is no specific late fee, but continued non-filing leads to dissolution.
