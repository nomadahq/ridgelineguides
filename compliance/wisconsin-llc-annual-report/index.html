Wisconsin LLC Annual Report: Deadline, Cost, and Filing Guide (2026)

Last updated: February 2026 · Applies to: Wisconsin LLCs

Wisconsin LLCs must file an annual report each year with the Department of Financial Institutions. The fee is $25 and the deadline is at the end of the calendar quarter containing your formation anniversary.

When is it due?

The annual report is due by the end of the calendar quarter in which your LLC formation anniversary falls.

How much does it cost?

The annual report fee is $25.

How to file

Filing steps

Go to Wisconsin DFI Corporations
Search for your LLC
File the annual report
Pay the $25 fee
Save confirmation

What happens if you miss the deadline?

Failure to file leads to administrative dissolution.

Do you need a registered agent in Wisconsin?

Yes. Wisconsin requires all LLCs to maintain a registered agent with a Wisconsin address.

Need a registered agent in Wisconsin?

A registered agent keeps your LLC in good standing and ensures you receive all legal and state documents on time.

Frequently asked questions

What is the anniversary quarter?

The quarter containing your LLC formation date. Formed in May = Q2 deadline (June 30).

Can I file online?

Yes. File through the Wisconsin DFI website.

Is the filing with the Secretary of State?

No. Wisconsin LLCs file with the Department of Financial Institutions (DFI).
