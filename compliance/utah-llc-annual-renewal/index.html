Utah LLC Annual Renewal: Deadline, Cost, and Filing Guide (2026)

Last updated: February 2026 · Applies to: Utah LLCs

Utah LLCs must file an annual renewal each year by their formation anniversary. At $18, Utah has one of the lowest annual fees in the country.

Quick summary

ItemDetails
FilingAnnual Renewal
FrequencyEvery year
Due dateBy the anniversary month of LLC formation
Filing fee$18
Late feeAdministrative dissolution after non-filing
Where to fileUtah Division of Corporations (utah.gov)
Consequence of non-filingAdministrative dissolution

When is it due?

Your annual renewal is due by the anniversary month of your LLC formation.

How much does it cost?

The annual renewal fee is $18 — one of the lowest in the country.

How to file

Filing steps

Go to Utah Business Entity Search
Search for your LLC
File the annual renewal
Pay the $18 fee
Save confirmation

What happens if you miss the deadline?

Failure to renew leads to administrative dissolution.

Do you need a registered agent in Utah?

Yes. Utah requires all LLCs to maintain a registered agent with a Utah address.

Need a registered agent in Utah?

A registered agent keeps your LLC in good standing and ensures you receive all legal and state documents on time.
