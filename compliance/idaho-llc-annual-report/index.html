Idaho LLC Annual Report: Deadline, Cost, and Filing Guide (2026)

Last updated: February 2026 · Applies to: Idaho LLCs

Idaho LLCs must file an annual report each year by their formation anniversary. Idaho is one of the few states that charges no fee for the annual report, making it purely an information update.

Quick summary

ItemDetails
FilingAnnual Report
FrequencyEvery year
Due dateBy the anniversary month of LLC formation
Filing feeNo fee (free)
Late feeAdministrative dissolution after 60+ days delinquent
Where to fileIdaho Secretary of State (sosbiz.idaho.gov)
Consequence of non-filingAdministrative dissolution

When is it due?

The annual report is due by the end of the anniversary month of your LLC formation each year.

How much does it cost?

Idaho charges no fee for the LLC annual report. This is one of the few states with a free annual filing.

How to file

Filing steps

Go to Idaho SOS Business
Search for your LLC
File the annual report
No payment required
Save confirmation

What happens if you miss the deadline?

If more than 60 days delinquent, the LLC may be administratively dissolved. Reinstatement requires filing overdue reports.

Do you need a registered agent in Idaho?

Yes. Idaho requires all LLCs to maintain a registered agent with an Idaho street address.

Need a registered agent in Idaho?

A registered agent keeps your LLC in good standing and ensures you receive all legal and state documents on time.

Frequently asked questions

Is there really no fee?

Correct. Idaho does not charge for LLC annual reports.

What information is on the report?

Business name, registered agent, principal office address, and member/manager information.

Can I file online?

Yes. File through the Idaho Secretary of State business portal.
