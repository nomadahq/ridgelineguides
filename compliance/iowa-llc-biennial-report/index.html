Iowa LLC Biennial Report: Deadline, Cost, and Filing Guide (2026)

Last updated: February 2026 · Applies to: Iowa LLCs

Iowa LLCs must file a biennial report every two years. The filing fee is $60. Most LLCs file in odd-numbered years, but your specific cycle depends on your formation date.

When is it due?

The biennial report is due April 1 of your assigned filing year (odd or even, based on formation).

How much does it cost?

The biennial report fee is $60. A $50 late penalty applies to late filings.

How to file

Filing steps

Go to Iowa FAST system
Search for your LLC
File the biennial report
Pay the $60 fee
Save confirmation

What happens if you miss the deadline?

Late filings are accepted with a $50 penalty. Continued non-filing leads to administrative dissolution.

Do you need a registered agent in Iowa?

Yes. Iowa requires all LLCs to maintain a registered agent with an Iowa address.

Need a registered agent in Iowa?

A registered agent keeps your LLC in good standing and ensures you receive all legal and state documents on time.

Frequently asked questions

Is it annual or biennial?

Biennial — every 2 years.

How do I know my filing year?

Check with the Iowa Secretary of State. Your cycle depends on your formation date.

Can I file online?

Yes. File through the Iowa FAST system.
