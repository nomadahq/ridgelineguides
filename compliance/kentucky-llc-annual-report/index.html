Kentucky LLC Annual Report: Deadline, Cost, and Filing Guide (2026)

Last updated: February 2026 · Applies to: Kentucky LLCs

Kentucky LLCs must file an annual report by June 30 each year. At $15, Kentucky has one of the lowest annual report fees in the country.

Quick summary

ItemDetails
FilingAnnual Report
FrequencyEvery year
Due dateJune 30
Filing fee$15
Late feePenalty for late filing; administrative dissolution risk

Consequence of non-filingAdministrative dissolution

When is it due?

The annual report is due June 30 each year for all Kentucky LLCs.

How much does it cost?

The filing fee is $15 — one of the lowest in the country.

How to file

Filing steps

Go to Kentucky SOS Business
Search for your LLC
File the annual report
Pay the $15 fee
Save confirmation

What happens if you miss the deadline?

Late filing results in penalties. Continued non-filing leads to administrative dissolution.

Do you need a registered agent in Kentucky?

Yes. Kentucky requires all LLCs to maintain a registered agent with a Kentucky address.

Need a registered agent in Kentucky?

A registered agent keeps your LLC in good standing and ensures you receive all legal and state documents on time.

Frequently asked questions

Yes. Kentucky has one of the lowest annual report fees in the country.

June 30 each year, regardless of formation date.

Yes. File through the Kentucky Secretary of State website.
