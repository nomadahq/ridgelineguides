Hawaii LLC Annual Report: Deadline, Cost, and Filing Guide (2026)

Last updated: February 2026 · Applies to: Hawaii LLCs

Hawaii LLCs must file an annual report through Hawaii Business Express. Hawaii has one of the lowest annual report fees in the country at just $15. The report is due by the end of the calendar quarter in which your LLC was formed.

When is it due?

Your annual report is due by the end of the calendar quarter in which your LLC anniversary falls. For example, if formed in February (Q1), your report is due by March 31 each year.

How much does it cost?

The annual report fee is just $15 — one of the lowest in the country.

How to file

Filing steps

Go to Hawaii Business Express
Log in and select your LLC
File the annual report
Pay the $15 fee
Save confirmation

What happens if you miss the deadline?

Failure to file leads to administrative dissolution. Reinstatement requires filing overdue reports and paying fees.

Do you need a registered agent in Hawaii?

Yes. Hawaii requires all LLCs to maintain a registered agent with a Hawaii street address.

Need a registered agent in Hawaii?

A registered agent keeps your LLC in good standing and ensures you receive all legal and state documents on time.

Frequently asked questions

Why is the fee so low?

Hawaii charges one of the lowest annual report fees in the country at $15.

How do I know my quarter?

Q1 = Jan-Mar, Q2 = Apr-Jun, Q3 = Jul-Sep, Q4 = Oct-Dec. Your due quarter matches when your LLC was formed.

Can I file online?

Yes. File through Hawaii Business Express.
