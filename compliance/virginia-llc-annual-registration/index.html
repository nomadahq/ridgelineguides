Virginia LLC Annual Registration Fee: Deadline, Cost, and Filing Guide (2026)

Last updated: February 2026 · Applies to: Virginia LLCs

Virginia requires every LLC to pay an annual registration fee to the State Corporation Commission (SCC). Virginia is unique in that this is technically a fee payment rather than an informational report — you pay $50 to maintain your LLC's active status. If your information has changed, you file a separate amendment.

When is it due?

The annual registration fee is due by the last day of the month in which your LLC was formed.

How much does it cost?

The annual registration fee is $50. If paid late, Virginia assesses a penalty (typically 10%, or $5) plus interest.

How to file

Filing steps

Go to the Virginia SCC website
Log in or search for your LLC
Select the option to pay your annual registration fee
Pay the $50 fee
Save your confirmation

What happens if you don't file?

If you fail to pay for two consecutive years, the SCC may terminate your LLC. Each officer or director may become personally liable for debts incurred after termination. Reinstatement requires paying all past-due fees, penalties, and interest.

Do you need a registered agent in Virginia?

Yes. Virginia requires every LLC to maintain a registered agent with a physical Virginia street address.

Need a registered agent in Virginia?

A professional registered agent ensures you receive all legal and state documents on time and keeps your LLC in good standing.

Virginia LLC compliance checklist

Ongoing requirements for Virginia LLCs

Pay annual registration fee by your anniversary month ($50)
Maintain a registered agent with a Virginia street address
File any amendments if LLC information has changed
File Virginia income tax return (Form 502 for pass-through entities)
File federal tax return (Form 1065 or Schedule C)
Collect and remit Virginia sales tax if applicable

Frequently asked questions

Is this an annual report or just a fee?

Virginia's requirement is technically a fee payment, not an informational report. You pay $50 to keep your LLC active. Information changes require a separate amendment.

What is the BPOL tax?

The Business, Professional and Occupational License (BPOL) tax is a local tax imposed by many Virginia cities and counties. It varies by locality.

Can I pay online?

Yes. Virginia's SCC allows online payment through their website.
