New Mexico LLC: No Annual Report Required (2026)

Last updated: February 2026 · Applies to: New Mexico LLCs

New Mexico does not require LLCs to file an annual report or pay an annual fee. This makes New Mexico one of the most business-friendly states for LLC compliance. However, you must still maintain a registered agent.

When is it due?

New Mexico does not require any annual filing for LLCs.

How much does it cost?

No annual fee of any kind.

How to file

Filing steps

No annual filing required
Maintain your registered agent
File amendments if business information changes
Keep operating agreement current

What happens if you miss the deadline?

Since there is no annual report, there is nothing to miss.

Do you need a registered agent in New Mexico?

Yes. Even without an annual report, New Mexico requires all LLCs to maintain a registered agent with a New Mexico address.

Need a registered agent in New Mexico?

A registered agent keeps your LLC in good standing and ensures you receive all legal and state documents on time.

Frequently asked questions

Does New Mexico really have no annual report?

Correct. New Mexico is one of the few states with absolutely no annual filing requirement for LLCs.

Do I still need a registered agent?

Yes. A registered agent is required regardless.

Are there any state taxes?

New Mexico has a gross receipts tax (similar to sales tax) and state income tax, but no annual LLC fee.
