Oklahoma LLC Annual Certificate: Deadline, Cost, and Filing Guide (2026)

Last updated: February 2026 · Applies to: Oklahoma LLCs

Oklahoma LLCs must file an annual certificate each year by their formation anniversary. The fee is $25.

Quick summary

ItemDetails
FilingAnnual Certificate
FrequencyEvery year
Due dateBy the anniversary of LLC formation
Filing fee$25
Late feeAdministrative dissolution after non-filing
Where to fileOklahoma Secretary of State (sos.ok.gov)
Consequence of non-filingAdministrative dissolution

When is it due?

The annual certificate is due by the anniversary of your LLC formation each year.

How much does it cost?

The filing fee is $25.

How to file

Filing steps

Go to Oklahoma SOS Business
Search for your LLC
File the annual certificate
Pay the $25 fee
Save confirmation

What happens if you miss the deadline?

Failure to file leads to administrative dissolution.

Do you need a registered agent in Oklahoma?

Yes. Oklahoma requires all LLCs to maintain a registered agent with an Oklahoma address.

Need a registered agent in Oklahoma?

A registered agent keeps your LLC in good standing and ensures you receive all legal and state documents on time.

Frequently asked questions

What is an annual certificate?

It is Oklahoma's version of an annual report — a simple filing to confirm your LLC information.

Can I file online?

Yes. File through the Oklahoma Secretary of State website.

Is there a grace period?

Contact the SOS for specific grace period rules.
