Rhode Island LLC Annual Report: Deadline, Cost, and Filing Guide (2026)

Last updated: February 2026 · Applies to: Rhode Island LLCs

Rhode Island LLCs must file an annual report each year. The fee is $50. Filing is done through the Secretary of State business portal.

Quick summary

ItemDetails
FilingAnnual Report
FrequencyEvery year
Due dateBy the anniversary of LLC formation
Filing fee$50
Late feeRevocation after continued non-filing
Where to fileRhode Island Secretary of State (sos.ri.gov)
Consequence of non-filingRevocation of LLC status

When is it due?

Your annual report is due by the anniversary of your LLC formation each year.

How much does it cost?

The annual report fee is $50.

How to file

Filing steps

Go to RI SOS Business Portal
Search for your LLC
File the annual report
Pay the $50 fee
Save confirmation

What happens if you miss the deadline?

Continued non-filing leads to revocation of LLC status.

Do you need a registered agent in Rhode Island?

Yes. Rhode Island requires all LLCs to maintain a registered agent with a Rhode Island address.

Need a registered agent in Rhode Island?

A registered agent keeps your LLC in good standing and ensures you receive all legal and state documents on time.

Frequently asked questions

Can I file online?

Yes. File through the Rhode Island Secretary of State business portal.

Registered agent, office address, and member information.

Risk of revocation of your LLC status.
