Nebraska LLC Biennial Report: Deadline, Cost, and Filing Guide (2026)

Last updated: February 2026 · Applies to: Nebraska LLCs

Nebraska LLCs must file a biennial report every two years. The fee is $26 and the deadline is April 1 of odd-numbered years.

Quick summary

ItemDetails

Consequence of non-filingAdministrative dissolution

When is it due?

The biennial report is due April 1 of odd-numbered years (2027, 2029, etc.).

How much does it cost?

The biennial report fee is $26.

How to file

Filing steps

Go to Nebraska SOS Business Services
Search for your LLC
File the biennial report
Pay the $26 fee
Save confirmation

What happens if you miss the deadline?

Failure to file leads to administrative dissolution.

Do you need a registered agent in Nebraska?

Yes. Nebraska requires all LLCs to maintain a registered agent with a Nebraska address.

Need a registered agent in Nebraska?

A registered agent keeps your LLC in good standing and ensures you receive all legal and state documents on time.

Frequently asked questions

Biennial — every 2 years in odd-numbered years.

Yes. File through the Nebraska Secretary of State website.
